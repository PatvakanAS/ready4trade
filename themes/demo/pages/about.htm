{{ 'Ready4Trade'|_ }} {{ 'Central Asia'|_ }}

{{ 'Ready4Trade Central Asia is a four-year EU-funded project, which aims to promote intra-regional and international trade in five Central Asian countries – Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan. This will be achieved by enhancing the transparency of cross-border requirements, removing regulatory and procedural barriers, strengthening business capability to comply with trade formalities and standards, as well as by improving cross-border e-commerce.'|_ }}

{{ 'Ready4Trade Central Asia is the third component in the framework of a larger EU-funded programme aimed to support Investment, Competitiveness and Trade in Central Asia.'|_ }}

{{ 'Although Ready4Trade Central Asia will reap synergies from regional implementation, the ensuing technical interventions will respond to the individual needs and priorities of each beneficiary country.'|_ }}

{{ 'The project is implemented by the International Trade Centre in close collaboration with national partners.'|_ }}
